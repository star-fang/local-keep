==General Info==

'''Technologies''' are researched in the [[Buildings/Academy|Academy]].

{{TOC limit|1}}

Technologies can be classified as either Economic or Military Technology. Economic technologies contain buffs and unlocks for various non-military aspects of your city. Military technologies contain buffs to military troops and unlock higher tier units.
